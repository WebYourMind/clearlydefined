# How to curate

Go to clearlydefined.io/definitions.

![clearlydefined.io](https://github.com/clearlydefined/clearlydefined/blob/master/docs/image4.png)

Search for the component you’re interested in and identify the thing you want to change.

![clearlydefined.io](https://github.com/clearlydefined/clearlydefined/blob/master/docs/image2.png)

Make your curation.

![clearlydefined.io](https://github.com/clearlydefined/clearlydefined/blob/master/docs/image5.png)

Press the “contribute” button and include your justification on the popup. When you click “OK” the curation will be submitted and a PR will be created.

![clearlydefined.io](https://github.com/clearlydefined/clearlydefined/blob/master/docs/image1.png)

Review PR on GitHub and participate in discussion if applicable. 

![clearlydefined.io](https://github.com/clearlydefined/clearlydefined/blob/master/docs/image3.png)
